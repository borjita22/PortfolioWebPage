WHO AM I?

Hello again! My name is Borja García and I´m a videogame and web developer. I was born in Asturias, in a city called Oviedo in 1998. Since I was a child I have always considered myself a person with a great imagination. That was one of the main reasons, along with my passion for video games and technology, that led me to want, from a very early age, to enter the world of video game development.

I studied computer engineering at the University of Oviedo for three years, but after realizing that that was not for me, I decided to delve fully into the programming of interactive virtual experiences, finishing my degree studies in video game design and development at UDIT, a Madrid university. After that, I did a master's degree in Big Data for business in Universidad Alfonso X El Sabio, also in Madrid, and I have recently entered the world of web design and development as a way to expand my work area and my knowledge.
